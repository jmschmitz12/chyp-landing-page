About Chyp

Did you know that 70% of golfers encounter the beverage cart only one time per round, and that 44% of golfers report regularly missed opportunities to be serviced during a round of play?

At Chyp, our mission is to cover this gap between your golf course's beverage cart service and your customers. Chyp aims to revolutionize the golf experience by ensuring that golfers and beverage cart attendants can connect seamlessly whenever possible. By doing so, we're addressing the pain points that golfers face when they miss out on food and drink services during their round.

In less than 15 seconds, your customers can send a no-obligation service request to your beverage cart staff, making better use of your team's time and resources, while enhancing your customers' experience.

How Chyp Works

At Chyp we understand that staffing the beverage cart isn't always efficient, and on-course deliveries can slow down the rate of play. . .

Chyp doesn't require any POS integration, GPS tracking, or inventory management. The beauty of Chyp lies in its simplicity! Golfers scan a QR code and will be taken to your course's request page. They'll then enter and submit basic information such as their name, hole number and cart number. Your staff will get a notification in real time about the order. If ready and able, the beverage cart attendant can navigate to the customer for service. It's that easy! And if there is no beverage cart staffed, or if course conditions don't allow for on-demand delivery, no problem! The last thing you'd want is for customers to place orders that never arrive.

Chyp in Action
